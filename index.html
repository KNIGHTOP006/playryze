Tournament Format

HOW IT
WORKS

01
Round 1 — Group Stage
25 teams split into 5 groups of 5. Full round-robin within each group — every team plays 4 matches. Top 2 teams from each group advance. 7-minute halves.
02
Round 2 — Super Groups
10 qualifiers re-seeded into 2 groups of 5. Round-robin again — every team plays 4 more matches. Top 2 from each super group advance to semis.
03
Round 3 — Semi Finals
Group A winner vs Group B runner-up. Group B winner vs Group A runner-up. 10-minute halves. Penalty shootout if level after full time.
04
Round 4 — The Final
The two semi-final winners battle for the championship. 15-minute halves + extra time + penalties if needed. Trophy ceremony + prize distribution on court.
MATCH RULES

Kit RequirementsMatching jerseys

Turf RulesTurf shoes or flat-soled sports shoes only. No metal studs.

Fair PlayYellow/Red card system.yellow = 5 min off field, 2 yellows = red. Red card = banned next match.
✓
Scoring3 pts win · 1 pt draw · 0 pt loss. Goal difference as tiebreaker, then head-to-head.

Our Mission

BUILDING
INDIA'S NEXT
SPORTS ERA

PlayRyze was born from one belief: that India's youth athletes deserve more than disorganized local tournaments. We're building the infrastructure, experience, and community that turns grassroots football into a launch pad for the next generation of Indian sports stars.

Starting in Bengaluru with 5v5 turf football, we're expanding into cricket, basketball, and athletics — creating a multi-sport ecosystem where every young athlete gets a fair, professional, and exhilarating stage to perform.
